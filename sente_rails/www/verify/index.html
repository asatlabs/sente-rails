How does this verification work?

The MDA issued this receipt to Sente Rails the moment the aggregator confirmed your payment. The same record sits in the MDA's audit log. If anyone alters either copy, the two would no longer match — and any officer would know.

Sente Rails never holds public funds. The aggregator settles directly to each MDA's collection account under PFMA §43.
