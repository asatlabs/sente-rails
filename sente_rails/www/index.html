What it stands for

Sovereign by design. Composable by default.

⚖️

Sovereignty

Apache 2.0, source-available, deployable on Ugandan-owned infrastructure. No vendor lock, no offshore data residency, no royalty meter. The Ministry of ICT can fork it tomorrow.

🔌

Composability

Public REST /v1 surface. UGHub-ready integration adapters per MDA. Every payment route, every identity provider, every fiscal endpoint is a swappable adapter — not a fork.

⚡

Velocity

14 days from MoU to a live counter. Three verticals already modelled (Gulu City trading licence, Lands title transfer, URSB business registration). A new MDA is a Country Profile entry and a service catalog row, not a six-month rebuild.

Three demo verticals

The same rail, three government workflows.

Local Government

Trading Licence

Gulu City · MDA mode A

Citizen renews a trading licence at the City Hall counter. NIRA identity lookup, fee assessment, MTN MoMo payment, fiscal receipt — under 90 seconds.

Title Transfer

Ministry of Lands · MDA mode B

Multi-MDA assessment: stamp duty (URA) + registration fee (MoL) on one Payment Intent, split at the aggregator under PFMA §43. One receipt, two ledgers.

Business Registration

URSB · MDA mode A
